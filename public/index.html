MKE Pickup Alerts

Get a text the night before your City of Milwaukee garbage and/or recycling pickup!

What this does

We look up the official City of Milwaukee collection schedule for your address and:

  • Send a reminder text the night before garbage and/or recycling pickup.
  • Let you text STATUS any time to see your next pickup dates.
  • Let you stop messages instantly with CANCEL.

How signup works

  1. Enter your mobile number and Milwaukee address in the form on the right.
  2. We validate your address against the city’s collection service.
  3. You’ll get a text asking you to confirm – reply YES to turn alerts on.

If the address can’t be matched to a valid collection schedule, we’ll tell you instead of signing you up.

Texts you can send

  • Confirm alerts: reply YES (or Y) to the first message.
  • Check your schedule: after you’re active, send STATUS (or any message other than CANCEL) to get your next pickup dates and a reminder of how to opt out.
  • Stop alerts: send CANCEL at any time to unsubscribe. You’ll get a confirmation text and no further reminders.

Your phone number is only used to send these pickup reminders and respond to your commands. You can opt out whenever you like by texting CANCEL.
